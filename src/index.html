General FAQ
What are the dates and hours of the festival?
↓
Smokin' Grooves takes place June 19th, 2022 at LA State Historic Park in Los Angeles, California. Festival hours are 11AM to 11PM.
Are there lockers available for rent?
↓
Lockers will be offered in both the GA and VIP areas upon arrival at the festival.
Can I leave and re-enter on the same day?
↓
Re-entry is not permitted.
What is the bag policy?
↓
NO backpacks, purses or bags are allowed to be brought into the venue
How do I contact the festival?
↓
For general festival questions, email us at info@smokingroovesfestival.com
For more help
↓
For more help, please visit the official Smokin Grooves Festival FAQ site
